Our menu

Premium coffee, freshly prepared and ready to go. Choose your favorite espresso drink or take home our freshly roasted beans to enjoy anytime.

☕ Espresso

Bold. Pure. Intense.
Our Espresso is the heart of every great coffee. Crafted from finely ground premium beans and extracted to perfection, each shot delivers a rich aroma, velvety crema, and deep, complex flavor.

☕ Cappuccino

Smooth. Frothy. Comforting.
A timeless favorite, our Cappuccino blends bold espresso with steamed milk and a thick, airy layer of foam. Balanced and creamy, it’s finished with delicate latte art for a touch of beauty in every cup.

☕ Latte

Creamy. Smooth. Relaxing.
Our Latte combines rich espresso with silky steamed milk for a mellow, creamy experience. With a light layer of foam on top, it’s smooth, comforting, and easy to enjoy any time of day.

☕ Mocha

Rich. Chocolatey. Indulgent. For those who crave something sweet, our Mocha blends bold espresso with premium chocolate and steamed milk. Topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate, it’s the perfect balance of coffee and cocoa.
